## Interpretation

- **S1 / S2 (single task):** A and B differ only by the dispatch delta (S4). A is leaner because it has no router layer.
- **S3 (both modes):** B loads one router + two mode files; A loads two mode SKILLs. If the router is cheaper than the second mode SKILL it replaces, B breaks even or wins here.
- **S4 (dispatch overhead):** the pure structural cost of choosing B over A.
- **Baseline:** the integration removes the six-way duplication. The reduction is the headline value of this work, independent of A vs B.
